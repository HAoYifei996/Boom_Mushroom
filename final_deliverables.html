Abstract

In thousands years ago, there is an island called Mushroom Island...

Only the bravest worriors are sent to this island to fight until the last man stand.

Are you brave enough? If your answer is yes, Come on then.

The glory awaits...

Game specials

Enough of story-telling, here is the game specials we made in this project.

  • We create random Muzzle shake to enhance shooting experience.
  • The mushrooms are not just decorations. Try to shoot some of them.
  • You can choose either fast shooting mode or tap shooting mode. The choice is yours.
  • Double harm when hitting the head.
  • Crouch down by shrinking size!
  • Sending messages to your friends in the same room!

    • Future Directions

    Due to time limits, there are still a lot we want to cover in this project that we haven't implemented. Potential future directions are listed below:

  • More game modes: We couldn't cover more game modes in this game. Currently it's and endless FPS game with no winners. Players will imediatelly revive when dead. A various game modes could be a huge step up for this game.
  • Choices of roles and guns: We plans to add more roles and guns for players to choose from. Unfortunately it turns out there is more work than we expected. So we leave this as a future improving directions.
